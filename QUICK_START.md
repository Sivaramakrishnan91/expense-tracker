# 🚀 Personal Finance Tracker — Quick Start Guide

## Getting Started in 3 Steps

### Step 1: Open the Application
Double-click `index.html` to open the Personal Finance Tracker in your browser.

### Step 2: Add a Transaction
1. Fill in the form at the top:
   - **Date**: Today's date (pre-filled)
   - **Type**: Select "Income" or "Expenditure"
   - **Name**: e.g., "Salary" or "Groceries"
   - **Amount**: Enter the amount in ₹
   - **Category**: Select from dropdown (changes based on type)
   - **Notes**: Optional additional information

2. Click "Add Transaction"

### Step 3: View Your Data
- **Dashboard**: See totals at the top
- **Transactions Table**: View all entries below
- **Category Breakdown**: See spending by category at the bottom

## 📥 Try Sample Data

Want to see the app in action? Import the sample data:

1. Click "📥 Import" button
2. Select `sample-data.xlsx` from the expense-tracker folder
3. Click "Confirm" to import

This will load 13 sample transactions for July 2026.

## 🎯 Key Features to Try

### Filters
- **Date Range**: Click "This Month" dropdown to change period
- **Type**: Filter by Income or Expenditure
- **Category**: Filter by specific category
- **Search**: Type to search transaction names

### Sorting
- Use the "Sort by" dropdown to reorder transactions
- Options: Date, Amount, Name (ascending/descending)

### Edit/Delete
- Click "✏️ Edit" to modify a transaction
- Click "🗑️ Delete" to remove (with confirmation)

### Export
- Click "📤 Export" to download your data as Excel
- File includes both transactions and summary sheets

## 💡 Tips

1. **Data is Auto-Saved**: Everything saves automatically to your browser
2. **No Internet Needed**: Works completely offline
3. **Mobile Friendly**: Open on your phone for on-the-go tracking
4. **Regular Backups**: Export monthly for backup

## 📊 Understanding the Dashboard

- **Total Income** (Green): Sum of all income transactions
- **Total Expenses** (Red): Sum of all expenditure transactions
- **Net Balance** (Blue/Red): Income minus Expenses
  - Blue = Positive balance
  - Red = Negative balance (spending more than earning)

## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: In your browser's local storage. It's private and secure.

**Q: Can I use this on multiple devices?**
A: Export from one device and import on another to sync data.

**Q: What if I close the browser?**
A: Your data is saved automatically and will be there when you return.

**Q: Can I delete all data?**
A: Clear your browser's local storage or delete transactions individually.
